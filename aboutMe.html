About Me ☺️

Hola, hola! I’m Barbara Griffin but friends call me Barbie. I consider myself a designer that loves to bring cute and fun designs through coding and illustration. Everything started at the University of Miami, where after creating digital designs, using game engines, and started to explore programming I decided to dual-major in Interactive Media and Graphic Design. My love for video games made me immerse myself in the world of interactive media, and the idea of using my own art to create an engaging design that brings a smile to someone, motivates me to continue in this interesting world.
